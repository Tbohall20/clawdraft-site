Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Clawdraft ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit clawdraft.app or purchase our products. We take your privacy seriously and keep data collection to the minimum necessary to operate.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what's needed to deliver your product and provide support:

  • Purchase information: Name, email address, and billing details — collected and processed by our payment provider at checkout.
  • Onboarding information: If you purchase a DFY package, you'll optionally provide details about your niche, content goals, and style preferences via our intake form. This is used solely to generate your personalized setup.
  • Usage data: We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first, cookieless analytics tool — to understand site traffic. No personal data is collected. No cookies are set.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your purchase and deliver your product
  • To send transactional emails (order confirmation, product delivery)
  • To provide customer support if you contact us
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Paddle — Payment processing and tax compliance. Paddle acts as the Merchant of Record and handles all payment data under their own privacy policy.
  • Formspree — Form submission handling for onboarding intake. Submissions are stored temporarily for processing.
  • Plausible Analytics — Cookieless, privacy-first website analytics. No personal data collected.

4. Data Retention

We retain your email address and purchase record for up to 3 years for support and legal compliance purposes. Onboarding intake data is deleted after your setup is delivered. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Our analytics provider (Plausible) is cookieless by design. No consent banner needed.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@clawdraft.app.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Payment data is never stored on our servers — it is handled entirely by our payment processor.

8. Children's Privacy

Clawdraft is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email where applicable. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach us at hello@clawdraft.app.
